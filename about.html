Our story

Hi, I'm Dexter.

I'm a Fort Wayne-based web developer with a CS degree and a genuine passion for building things on the web. I started Fort Wayne Web Studio because I kept seeing the same problem: talented local business owners with great products and services, but no online presence — or worse, an outdated, slow website that was actively hurting them.

Traditional agencies charge thousands up front and lock you into long contracts. DIY website builders look cheap and limit your ability to grow. There had to be a better way.

Fort Wayne Web Studio is that better way. I build custom, hand-coded websites — no templates, no page builders — deployed on Cloudflare's global network for blazing-fast performance. You get agency-quality work at a price that actually makes sense for a small business.

Every site I build is fully owned by you. Your code, your domain, your content. If you ever want to move on, you take everything with you. No lock-in, no gotchas.

You can see more of my personal work at dextermiller.com.

What we stand for

Our core values

Three principles guide every project we take on.

Affordability

Great websites shouldn't be a luxury. We keep our prices accessible so that a 10-person local business can look just as credible online as a national chain. No setup fees, no inflated monthly retainers.

Transparency

You'll always know exactly what you're paying for, what's being built, and when it will be done. No surprise invoices, no hidden fees, no confusing jargon. Just clear, honest communication from start to finish.

Quality

Affordable doesn't mean cheap. Every site is hand-coded, mobile-first, SEO-optimized, and performance-tested. We don't ship something we wouldn't be proud to put our name on.
