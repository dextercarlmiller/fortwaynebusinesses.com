Cloudflare is the world's largest content delivery network — it powers a significant portion of internet traffic. Cloudflare Pages is their static site hosting platform.

We use it because it gives your site:

  • Blazing-fast load times — files are served from data centers worldwide, close to every visitor
  • Rock-solid uptime — Cloudflare's infrastructure has 99.99%+ availability
  • Free SSL (HTTPS) — your site is secure by default
  • DDoS protection — built-in security without extra cost
  • Lower cost — we pass the savings to you

The bottom line: your visitors get a faster, more reliable experience, and you spend less on hosting.
